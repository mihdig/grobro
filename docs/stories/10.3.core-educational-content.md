# Story 10.3: Core Educational Content

## Status
Approved

## Story
**As a** content creator for GroBro,
**I want** to write and publish core educational guides,
**so that** users have trusted, accurate information for plant care.

## Acceptance Criteria
1. Guides written for all key topics: diary maintenance, watering, light, environment, diagnostics
2. Each guide is reviewed for accuracy and legal compliance
3. Content avoids medical claims, consumption advice, and illegal activity instructions
4. Guides include practical examples and screenshots from GroBro app
5. "Maintaining Your Plant Diary" guide published
6. "Understanding Substrate Moisture" guide published
7. "Light Basics for Healthy Plants" guide published
8. "Recognizing Common Stress Signs" guide published
9. "Using Diagnostics and Debug Console" guide published
10. All guides display proper disclaimers

## Guides to Write
- Maintaining Your Plant Diary (Story 10.1 infrastructure)
- Understanding Substrate Moisture (finger test, weight method)
- Light Basics (distance, intensity, photoperiod)
- Common Stress Signs (visual guide)
- Using GroBro's AI Features
- Environmental Monitoring Best Practices
- Safety and Legal Compliance
