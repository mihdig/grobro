# Story 10.6: Content Moderation Guidelines

## Status
Approved

## Story
**As a** content moderator for GroBro,
**I want** clear internal guidelines for reviewing user-generated content and guides,
**so that** all content meets legal, safety, and quality standards.

## Acceptance Criteria
1. Internal moderation playbook document created
2. Guidelines for what content is prohibited:
   - Consumption instructions
   - Medical claims
   - Illegal activity encouragement
   - Dangerous practices (e.g., butane extraction)
   - Copyrighted material without permission
3. Guidelines for what content is allowed:
   - Plant diary techniques
   - Environmental monitoring
   - Diagnostics interpretation
   - General horticultural practices
4. Review checklist for new guides before publication
5. Process for handling reported content (future: user-generated)
6. Escalation path for legal review
7. Regular quarterly review of all published guides
8. Version control for guide updates with change log

## Dev Notes
- This is an internal process story, not user-facing features
- Output: `docs/content-moderation-guidelines.md`
- Required before allowing any user-generated content
