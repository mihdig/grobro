# Story 10.5: Safety and Legal Content

## Status
Approved

## Story
**As a** responsible app provider,
**I want** clear safety and legal information displayed throughout the app,
**so that** users understand their responsibilities and GroBro is protected legally.

## Acceptance Criteria
1. "Safety & Legal" guide in dedicated category
2. Content covers: local law compliance, user responsibility, data privacy
3. Safety topics: fire safety, electrical safety, ventilation, chemical handling
4. Legal disclaimers appear in all AI-generated content
5. Clear statement: "GroBro is a diary tool, not cultivation instructions"
6. Privacy policy integrated into guide system
7. Terms of service accessible from settings
8. First-time users must acknowledge legal disclaimer before creating plants
9. All environmental/diagnostic features show: "For informational purposes only"
10. Export data includes disclaimer about user responsibility for data security
